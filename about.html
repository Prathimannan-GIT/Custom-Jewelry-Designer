Our Heritage

Three generations of excellence

From a small family workshop to a global atelier, our journey spans over 75 years of unparalleled craftsmanship and innovation.

Timeline

A legacy of innovation

Our journey through decades of excellence, from humble beginnings to global recognition.

The Foundation (1948-1970)

Marcus Blackwood established our first workshop in Manhattan's Diamond District, specializing in hand-engraved signet rings and custom family crests.

Global Expansion (1971-1995)

Under Eleanor Blackwood's leadership, we expanded internationally, opening ateliers in London's Mayfair and Paris' Place Vendôme.

Modern Renaissance (1996-2020)

James Blackwood revolutionized our craft by integrating CAD technology while preserving traditional techniques, earning international design awards.

Contemporary Excellence (2021-Present)

Today, under Sophia Blackwood-Chen, we lead the industry in sustainable luxury and innovative jewelry technology.

Award-Winning Design (2023)

Recognized by the Global Luxury Council for outstanding achievement in bespoke jewelry design and client experience.

Innovation Lab (2025-Beyond)

Establishing a dedicated research facility to explore new alloys and setting techniques for the next generation of jewelry.

Traditions

Time-honored techniques

Heritage Alloy Formulation

Artisanal Stone Selection

Legacy Restoration
