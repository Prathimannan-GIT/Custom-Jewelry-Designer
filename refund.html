Policy

Refund Policy

Our return and refund policy for custom jewelry pieces and services.

Custom Jewelry Orders

Because each piece is custom-made to your specifications, custom jewelry orders are non-refundable once production has begun. We encourage you to review all design details carefully before approving your final design.

Design Approval Process

You will receive detailed design renderings and specifications for approval before production begins. Once you approve the design and production starts, the order becomes non-refundable.

Production Cancellation

If you need to cancel an order before production begins, we will refund your payment minus a 25% design and consultation fee. Once production has started, no refunds will be issued.

Defective Items

If you receive a piece that is defective or does not match the approved design specifications, please contact us within 7 days of receipt. We will repair or replace the item at our expense.

Shipping Damage

All items are insured during shipping. If your piece arrives damaged, please contact us immediately within 48 hours with photos of the damage. We will arrange for repair or replacement.

Resizing and Adjustments

One complimentary resizing is included within 30 days of delivery if the piece does not fit as specified. Additional resizing or adjustments will incur a fee based on the complexity of the work.

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable:

  • Custom design consultation fees
  • Rush order processing fees
  • Custom engraving services
  • Items that have been modified or altered by the customer

Refund Process

If your situation qualifies for a refund, we will process it within 14 business days of approval. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Exchanges

Exchanges are generally not available for custom pieces due to their unique nature. However, we may consider store credit for future purchases in exceptional circumstances.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Refund Policy, please contact us through our contact page.
